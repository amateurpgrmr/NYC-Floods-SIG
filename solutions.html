üîÑ Lessons from October 2025 Event

The October 30, 2025 flooding demonstrated critical vulnerabilities:
  • Basement Safety: Two fatalities in flooded basements highlight need for immediate basement dwelling reforms and backwater valve requirements
  • System Capacity: Peak rainfall of 6 in/hr vs. 1.75 in/hr system capacity proves infrastructure upgrades are essential, not optional
  • Early Warning: Despite advance warnings, localized intensity overwhelmed forecasts - hyperlocal monitoring needed
  • Climate Reality: 108-year record broken shows climate change is accelerating faster than previous models predicted

Key Takeaway: NYC must act now with unprecedented investment and urgency. The cost of inaction far exceeds the cost of adaptation.
